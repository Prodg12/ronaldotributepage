CR 7 Tribute Page

  • Cristiano Ronaldo is Born

    Cristiano Ronaldo was born to Maria Dolores dos Santos Aveiro and Jose Dinis Aveiro in Santo Antonio, Madeira, Portugal. His mother was a cook, and his father was a municipal gardener

    6 February 1985

  • Ronaldo Plays for His First Amateur Team

    Ronaldo played for Andorinha at the age of eight. Ronaldo's father was the kit man for the team. Cristiano Ronaldo started to play football as a result of his father's influence. His great talent was evident from a very young age.

    2 July 1993

  • Ronaldo Signs with Nacional

    As Ronaldo's talent began to be seen by people beyond his father, he struck a deal with local club Nacional. While at Nacional, his coach saw his great talent and committed himself to train Ronaldo to be a great football player. The intense training that he received at Nacional positioned him for greatness.

    2 June 1995

  • Sporting CP Recruits Ronaldo

    After a great run at Nacional, Cristiano Ronaldo got the opportunity of a three-day trial with Sporting CP. The team later decided to sign the young football player. The scouter that discovered Ronaldo suggested to the youth coach that he should sign Ronaldo. He played for the club's youth teams from 1997 to 2001.

    2 June 1997

  • Ronaldo Makes His Professional Debut

    Ronaldo was the only player to have ever, in one single season, played for Sporting's under-16, under-17 and under-18 teams, the B team, and the first team. On October 7, 2002, Ronaldo made his big debut in the Primeira Liga. Sporting was up against Moreirense. Cristiano Ronaldo scored two goals for Sporting as the match concluded in a 3-0 win..

    8 October 2002

  • Ronaldo Transfers to Manchester United

    Ronaldo was transferred to Manchester United for £12.24 million. The transfer came out of a pre-season friendly between Manchester United and Sporting Lisbon. Sporting Lisbon defeated Manchester United 3-1. Alex Ferguson, as well as players such as Patrice Evra and Rio Ferdinand, were impressed by Cristiano's skills

    19 July 2003

  • Cristiano Makes His Debut in the Premier League

    The Portuguese rising star made his debut in the Premier League on August 16, 2003. His debut was praised by George Best as the most exciting debut that he had ever witnessed. Manchester United defeated Bolton Wanderers 4-0.

    17 August 2003

  • Ronaldo is Called Up for UEFA Euro 2004

    On August 20, 2003, Ronaldo made his first senior appearance for his home country in a 1-0 win over Kazakhstan. This led to him being called up for UEFA Euro 2004. He scored his first international goal in a 2-1 group stage loss to greece

    12 June 2004

  • Cristiano Ronaldo Serves as Portugal's Captain

    After becoming 22 years old, the young football star captained Portugal for the first time in a friendly match against Brazil. The match was played on February 6, 2007. The baller was given the number 7 shirt for the first time just before Euro 2008.

    7 February 2007

  • Cristiano Ronaldo First Ballon d'Or win

    at 23, Ronaldo wins his first Ballon d'Or award

    December 02 2008

  • Ronaldo Transfers to Real Madrid

    After accomplishing so much at Manchester United, Ronaldo felt it was time to move on to a new team. During this time Manchester United accepted an offer from Real Madrid of £80 million. On July 6, Cristiano Ronaldo was presented to the world as a new player of Real Madrid. Ronaldo made his debut in La Liga on August 29, 2009.

    7 July 2009

  • Ronaldo Scores His 100th League Goal for Real Madrid

    While at Real Madrid, the footballer enjoyed more success than he did in the Premier League. Ronaldo was instrumental in the team winning their first La Liga title in four years. He gained his 100th league goal for Madrid over 92 matches.

    25 March 2012

  • Ronaldo is Named as the Portuguese All-Time Top Scorer

    Ronaldo's two goals against Cameroon in an international match on March 5, 2014, made the footballer eligible for the title of Portugal's all-time top scorer.

    6 March 2014

  • Ronaldo is Declared the Most Charitable Sportsperson in the World

    Ronaldo donated £5 million to the relief effort in Nepal. The funds were sent to help after a major earthquake in Nepal. The earthquake killed over 8,000 people and left many without shelter.

    2 January 2015

  • Ronaldo Becomes the First Footballer to Score 100 goals in the UEFA Club Competition

    The footballer scored two goals in an away match against Bayern Munich in the 2016-2017 UEFA Champions League quarter-final. These two goals led Ronaldo to be recorded in history as the first player to score 100 goals in UEFA Club Competition

    13 April 2017

  • Ronaldo wins his Fifth Ballon d'Or

    Cristiano Ronaldo wins the fifth Ballon d'or of his career to equal Lionel Messi's record

    December 7, 2017

  • Ronaldo Signs a 4-Year Contract with Italian Club Juventus

    Ronaldo signed a four-year contract with Juventus. The cost of the transfer was €100 million. There was an additional €12 million paid in fees and solidarity contributions to Ronaldo's youth clubs. Cristiano Ronaldo's transfer to Juventus is the highest-paid by an Italian club for a player over the age of 30.

    11 July 2018

  • Ronaldo Wins His First Trophy with Juventus

    On July 26, 2019, Ronaldo won his first trophy with Juventus. The trophy was from the 2018 Supercoppa Italiana. The footballer scored the game-winning goal from a header against A.C. Milan. It was the only goal of the match.

    27 July 2019

  • Ronaldo Returns to Manchester United

    On 27 August 2021, Manchester United announced they had reached an agreement with Juventus to re-sign Ronaldo, subject to agreement of personal terms, visa and medical. The transfer was for an initial £12.85 million, with a two-year contract plus an optional year, and was confirmed on 31 August

    August 27, 2021

  • Ronaldo Returns scores his 800th career goal

    On December 2, 2021, Cristiano Ronaldo scores his 800th career goal against arsenal

    August 27, 2021

  • Ronaldo is the Most followed Athlete on Social Media

    Ronaldo is followed by 500 million people on Instagram. This makes the footballer the most followed person on social media.

    21 November 2022
